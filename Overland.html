About Overland

"Overland" is a strategic survival game that plunges players into a post-apocalyptic world overrun by alien creatures and environmental hazards. Developed by Finji, "Overland" challenges players to navigate treacherous terrain, scavenge for resources, and make life-or-death decisions as they lead a group of survivors on a desperate journey across the United States. At its core, "Overland" is a game of resource management and strategic planning. Players must carefully balance their limited supplies of fuel, food, and ammunition as they traverse procedurally generated landscapes filled with dangers at every turn. Whether it's avoiding swarms of ravenous creatures, navigating through toxic wastelands, or scavenging for supplies in abandoned towns, every decision carries weight and consequences. One of the defining features of "Overland" is its emergent storytelling, where each playthrough offers a unique narrative experience shaped by the player's choices and actions. From chance encounters with fellow survivors to moral dilemmas that test their humanity, players must grapple with the complexities of survival in a world teetering on the brink of collapse. Visually, "Overland" adopts a minimalist art style characterized by vibrant colors and clean, geometric shapes. This aesthetic not only lends the game a distinctive look but also serves to highlight the stark beauty of the post-apocalyptic landscapes and the eerie presence of the alien creatures that inhabit them. Overall, "Overland" is a challenging and immersive survival experience that offers players a compelling blend of strategy, exploration, and narrative depth. With its gripping gameplay, atmospheric visuals, and emergent storytelling, it stands as a standout title in the post-apocalyptic survival genre.
